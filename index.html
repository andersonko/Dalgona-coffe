Dalgona Coffee Recipe

this yummy south korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires four ingreidents to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard - you feel like you've won the jackpot ,right?

ingreidents

  • instant coffee powder or expresso Grandules
  • hot water
  • milk
  • sugar

Dalgona making process

Step 1:combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water
add 2 tablespoons (tbsp) of instant coffee , 2 tbsp of sugar and 2 tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl
step 2:Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy
-->
Using an eletric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixtur until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
Step 3: serve with milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. serve with a straw and stir, stir, stir to enjoy
